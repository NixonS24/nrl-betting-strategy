# Hypothesis: H_003

## ID
H_003

## Logic
Referee assignment and the day of the week may influence home advantage. 
1. Home win rates vary by day (e.g. Friday vs Sunday), potentially reflecting different refereeing styles or crowd pressures.
2. Individual referees may have persistent biases (conscious or unconscious) toward home teams.

## Variables
- `date`
- `day_of_week`
- `referee`
- `home_team`
- `away_team`
- `result`
- `bk_implied_home`

## Success Metric
- Join match data with NRL.com referee assignments.
- Perform ANOVA on home win rate by day of week.
- Perform ANOVA on home win rate by individual referee (min games = 10).
- Report calibration error (actual HW% - implied HW%) per bucket.
- Require `p_value < 0.05` for significance.
- Treat result as exploratory if `n` is small.
