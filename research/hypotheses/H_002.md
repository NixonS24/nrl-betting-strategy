# Hypothesis: H_002

## ID
H_002

## Logic
Suncorp Stadium may function as a systematic **fade-home** venue in the modern sample rather than a generic home-advantage venue.

The candidate signal is that bookmaker pricing may still treat the home side as if Suncorp carries a standard home edge, while actual home win performance at this venue is weaker than implied by market prices. Because Suncorp is a multi-team venue with mixed home-team identities, this should be tested directly as a venue-specific pricing question rather than assumed from the broader venue-bias model.

The hypothesis is only worth promoting if the result survives proper filtering and does not collapse once team composition and sample window are made explicit.

## Variables
- `venue_name`
- `season`
- `home_team`
- `away_team`
- `bk_home_close`
- `bk_away_close`
- `bk_implied_home`
- `bk_implied_away`
- `result`

## Success Metric
- Show that `venue_name == 'Suncorp Stadium'` has a home win rate materially below bookmaker-implied home probability in the test window.
- Require `p_value < 0.05`.
- Require a usable sample size with explicit reporting of `n`.
- Report whether a simple fade-home rule at Suncorp produces positive ROI without temporal leakage.
- Treat the result as exploratory only if the test is in-sample.
